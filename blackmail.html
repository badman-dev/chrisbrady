Dishonored Mockup Level

WORK IN PROGRESS

Stealth, Interconnected

This is my first time creating a mockup level instead of a fully playable one. I'm using Garry's Mod as a base for this, as I'm able to leverage several community mods to adequately emulate the stealth and player abilities of Dishonored, as well as my own familiarity with the Hammer level editor made for the Source Engine.

I'm utilizing this list of user mods as strong stand-ins for Dishonored's mechanics. The rest, such as patrols and readables, I am recreating using level scripting with entities from Half-Life 2.

The level takes place during the events of the original Dishonored, in an extra mission in which Corvo infiltrates a City Watch office in order to eliminate a Captain attempting to blackmail the Loyalist Conspiracy.

The player's goals are to eliminate the captain and recover whatever dirt he possesses on the Loyalists. The player has the non-lethal elimination option of exposing the captain for ladder-climbing murder of his predecessor, which they perform by collecting stolen evidence and placing it on the desk of the officer in charge of the investigation.
